Biostimulators such as Radiesse and Sculptra are a popular treatment option at med spas like Sandy Key Medical in Pensacola, FL. What are Biostimulators Pensacola?

Biostimulators are a type of cosmetic treatment that uses injectable fillers to stimulate the body’s natural collagen production. Collagen is a protein that is responsible for providing structure and elasticity to the skin. As we age, our bodies produce less collagen, resulting in wrinkles, sagging skin, and other signs of aging.

Biostimulators such as Radiesse and Sculptra work by injecting a substance into the skin that stimulates the body’s production of collagen. This helps to restore the skin’s elasticity and firmness, reducing the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines

Radiesse Pensacola

Radiesse is a biostimulator that is made from calcium hydroxylapatite microspheres suspended in a gel. When injected into the skin, Radiesse provides an immediate lifting and firming effect while also stimulating collagen production. Over time, the body absorbs the gel and replaces it with its own collagen, resulting in long-lasting results.

Radiesse is a popular treatment for a variety of cosmetic concerns, including:

  • Nasolabial folds (smile lines)
  • Marionette lines (lines that run from the corners of the mouth to the chin)
  • Chin wrinkles
  • Pre-jowl folds
  • Cheek augmentation
  • Non-surgical nose job

The procedure typically takes less than an hour and can be performed with little to no downtime. Results are visible immediately after the treatment and can last up to 18 months.

Sculptra Pensacola

Sculptra is a biostimulator that is made from poly-L-lactic acid, a biocompatible and biodegradable substance that has been used in medical applications for many years. Sculptra works by stimulating collagen production in the skin, resulting in a gradual improvement in skin texture and volume.

Sculptra is a popular treatment for a variety of cosmetic concerns, including:

  • Deep facial wrinkles and folds
  • Hollow cheeks
  • Sunken temples
  • Loss of volume in the hands

Unlike other biostimulators, Sculptra requires a series of injections spaced out over several months. This gradual approach allows for natural-looking results that improve over time as the body’s collagen production increases.

The procedure typically takes less than an hour, and there is little to no downtime. Results are not immediately visible and can take several months to appear. However, Sculptra results can last up to two years, making it a popular choice for those looking for long-lasting results.

Benefits of Biostimulators

Biostimulators offer several benefits over other cosmetic treatments, including:

  • Natural-looking results: Biostimulators work by stimulating the body’s natural collagen production, resulting in a natural-looking improvement in skin texture and volume.
  • Long-lasting results: Biostimulators can provide long-lasting results, with some treatments lasting up to two years.
  • Minimal downtime: Most biostimulator treatments require little to no downtime, making them a convenient option for those with busy schedules.
  • Versatility: Biostimulators can be used to address a wide range of cosmetic concerns, from fine lines and wrinkles to hollow cheeks and sunken temples.
